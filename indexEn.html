About Lendchain

LendChain is a decentralized digital asset finance service platform. Currently provides digital asset-backed loans and money management business. The biggest bright spot is the entire borrowing process based on the blockchain through the smart contract to provide collateral management, post-loan monitoring, credit rating and other services using a fully automated platform. LendChain team has a domain name, loan platform, P2P financial management, lending institutions and work experience. Due to business attributes and needs we selected one of the world's successful blockchain companies - Gongxinbao - as our strategic partner, GXB main business is based on blockchain data trading platform. After LendChain products on the line there will be in-depth cooperation with GXB and the collaboration with Block City application will provide LendChain a solid base of loyal users in the shortest possible time.
